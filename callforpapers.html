Research and Scientific Innovation Society

Research and Scientific Innovation Society (RSIS International) is the leading International Professional non-profit making Society that promote the progress of research and innovation through International Conferences, Discussions, Seminars & to publish Professional International online journals, newsletters and to carry research and innovation at International level.

Research and Scientific Innovation Society is formed to promote latest academic and scientific research. Research and Scientific Innovation Society has maintained a mission statement that responds both to its own change, and to the new developments in the world.

Our main purpose and target are to serve as an organisation for stimulation, guiding and support its members to complete the general aim and responsibilities to build the research and development activities for the advancement of general welfare.

About JBRT

JBRT (Journal of Business Research and Technology) is a prestigious scholarly publication at the forefront of business and technology research.
